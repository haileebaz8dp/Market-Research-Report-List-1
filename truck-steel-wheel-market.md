<p><p>The truck steel wheel market is broadly classified into three types: casting, forging, and others. </p><p>Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds to create wheels, resulting in a cost-effective and versatile production method. Forging uses pressure to shape solid steel into wheels, providing superior strength and durability. Other methods may include machining or composite technologies, catering to specific performance requirements. Each type serves unique applications, balancing factors such as strength, weight, and manufacturing efficiency to meet diverse industry needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Basalt Fiber Products market is primarily divided into two types: basalt chopped fiber and basalt continuous fiber. Basalt chopped fiber consists of short, discrete fibers used in various applications for reinforcement, insulation, and composites, providing enhanced strength and thermal stability. In contrast, basalt continuous fiber is characterized by long, uninterrupted strands, making it ideal for high-performance applications, such as aerospace and automotive. Both types leverage the unique properties of basalt, including high resistance to heat and corrosion.</p></p>
